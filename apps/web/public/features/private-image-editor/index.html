Private image editor FAQ.

Does Obscura upload my images?

No. The v1 product is local-first and does not provide a backend upload endpoint.

Which edits are supported?

Crop, rotate, flip, resize, adjustments, filters, annotations, watermarking, and export.

Who is it for?

Personal creators who need fast private image preparation without desktop software.
