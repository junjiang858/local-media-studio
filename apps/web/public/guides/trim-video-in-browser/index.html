Video trimming FAQ.

Is this for long films?

No. Obscura v1 is tuned for short single-video editing, not long-form timelines.

Does trimming upload my clip?

No. The v1 workflow processes selected media in the browser.

Can I add subtitles too?

Yes. Obscura supports manual subtitle cues for short videos.
